# 🛒 Barrier-Free Smart Auto Shopping Cart

> Breaking down shopping barriers for people with disabilities, the elderly, and beyond.

## 📌 What is this?

Retail stores — big and small — are becoming increasingly automated, and the range of products keeps growing. For most people, this means more convenience. But for those with visual or hearing impairments, even using a simple self-checkout kiosk can be a real challenge. On top of that, finding a specific item among thousands of products is far from easy.

We noticed these difficulties that people with disabilities face when shopping at large retail stores, and decided to build a **barrier-free smart auto shopping cart** to make their shopping experience easier.

While the cart was primarily designed with visually impaired shoppers in mind, the bigger goal is to remove barriers across all generations — from children to the elderly — and bring barrier-free design closer to its true meaning.

## 🎯 Features

- Voice-controlled navigation
- Autonomous pathfinding (line tracer)
- Voice guidance + 3D-printed braille labels
- Collision detection (ultrasonic sensor)
- Instant RFID payment (items are charged when placed in the cart)
